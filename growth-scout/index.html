How Growth Scout works

      
    

    

      

        
What this is

        
Growth Scout watches European growth-stage companies and ranks them by live sourcing signals, so the names worth a look this week rise to the top.

      

      

        
How a score is built

        
Recent, strong signals add more points. Older signals fade out over weeks (that fading is the "decay"). Different signal types carry different point values (the "weights"): a fresh funding round counts for more than a press mention. Click any score to see its receipt.

      

      

        
Who writes what

        
Nothing here is written by the model. Every number comes from code and carries its source and pull time. The model only drafts prose, and you send everything yourself.
