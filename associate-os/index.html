How Associate OS works
Founder calls in, follow-through out. Associate OS turns every promise made on a call into a tracked commitment with a due date, a ready draft, and a nudge when it slips. The Meetings inbox on the left is home; Relationships, Pipeline, and the Monday brief build on the same data.
Every commitment cites its quote. Each item shows the verbatim transcript line it came from, and its due date is computed by code from the phrase used on the call ("by Friday", "early next week"). Older items escalate on their own. Click any quote to jump to that line in the transcript.
The agent drafts. Isaac sends. Nothing here is written by the model on its own: dates, statuses, and numbers come from code, and the model only drafts prose. No email leaves this app. Every draft has an Open in Gmail button so you review and hit send yourself.
