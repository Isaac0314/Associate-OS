Radar

Companies and people, one queue. A 30-second tour.

1 · Companies

The Companies tab ranks European growth-stage companies by live signals: open roles, Hacker News mentions, GitHub stars, founder posting cadence on X, and sourced funding events. Starring a company holds it on the Watch tab and pushes it into the shared pipeline. Founder names open that person on the People tab.

2 · People

The People tab ranks people who look like they are about to start a company. Every candidate is real, from three inflows: watched follows (the recent follows of your European VC scout accounts), Show HN authors, and, when connected, officers of newly incorporated UK companies from Companies House. Each is enriched from the live X, HN and Companies House APIs. Every person carries live numbers and a real link. There are no sample profiles.

3 · Scoring

Recent, strong signals add more points; older signals fade out over weeks. A company score is the score for this period of time, and the score history chart in the company panel shows how it moved over time. Click any score for its receipt. Every number comes from code and carries its source and pull time; the model only drafts prose.
